My Services

Do you have a story you’d like to tell, an experience from which you’ve gained invaluable insight or a brand, idea or organisation you wish to bring to life? If so, let’s collaborate!

Content Writing

As a craft, writing holds the power to sell, educate, impact, unite and bring change – sometimes even all at once. My expertise ranges from feature articles and blog posts to product descriptions and general website copy. Whatever the purpose behind your project, I am confident that together we can bring it to life in a truly unique way.

Social Media

There is no denying the scope of social media. In modern times, it’s proven itself to be a highly effective tool in the realms of activism, business and community building. Having said that, I believe authentic, personalised content generates the most engagement. I can work with you to build a tailored growth strategy, and consequently create both written and visual content to ensure your page thrives.

Dari Interpretation

Most of my written work is in English, however Dari is my mother tongue. While volunteering in Cyprus, I was able to offer translation support to the Afghan diaspora based there. I am especially interested in developing this service further, and am open to any urgent interpreting requests, as well as Dari Interpretation roles abroad.

S2 E6. Mariam - Volunteering with Afghans a massive wake up call

Mariam talks movingly and very honestly about her identity as a young woman from a displaced family and the journey that her volunteering set her on. As a Dari speaker, Mariam spent a lot of time serving Afghans at the Centre. This forced her to confront her own privilege and the pain of people who she shares a culture and history with.
